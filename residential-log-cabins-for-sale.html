About Log house

A log house, or log building, is a structure built with horizontal logs interlaced at the edges by scratching. Logs might be rounded, made even or hewn to various other forms, either handcrafted or crushed. The term "log cabin" typically refers to a smaller sized, a lot more rustic log house, such as a hunting cabin in the woods, that may or might not have power or pipes.
