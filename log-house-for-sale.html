About Log house

A log home, or log building, is a structure constructed with horizontal logs interlocked at the edges by notching. Logs might be rounded, made even or hewn to various other shapes, either handcrafted or grated. The term "log cabin" normally refers to a smaller sized, much more rustic log house, such as a searching cabin in the timbers, that might or might not have electrical power or pipes.
